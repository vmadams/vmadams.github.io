Things You Need to Know Before Coming to New Jersey

  • Pumping your own gas is illegal: New Jersey is one of the only states where attendants must pump your gas for you.
  • Driving too slow is also illegal: Keep up with the flow of traffic and avoid driving in the left lane unless passing.
  • New Jersey has the most diners out of all the states: It's known as the "Diner Capital of the World."
  • New Jersey has the most horses in all of the states: There are more horses per square mile here than anywhere else in the U.S.
